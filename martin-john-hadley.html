Martin John Hadley

Data Visualisation Consultant
Martin is a technical consultant and co-founder of Visible Data Ltd, specialising in data visualisation and is an instructor for a variety of online-learning platforms that include Lynda.com and SAGE Publishing.

Currently, Martin also works for University of Oxford as the Technical Lead for the Interactive Data Network (IDN); a service that supports researchers in developing interactive visualisations of their research data to improve engagement and impact.

He's an outspoken and enthusiastic evangelist for reproducible research and Open Data, having been invited to speak at a number of events and panels on the benefits of making research open.

Martin's preferred scripting language of choice is R, except in more pure math areas where he will switch to the Wolfram Language - having worked for three years as senior technical consultant for Wolfram Research using Mathematica.
